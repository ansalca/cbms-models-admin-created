What We Offer

Real-time Bus Tracking

Our system provides accurate and up-to-the-minute location tracking of college buses, enabling parents, students, and administrators to monitor bus movements on an interactive map. This feature ensures that stakeholders are informed about bus delays, route changes, or other disruptions.

Mobile & Desktop Interface

Our user-friendly mobile and web interfaces provide easy access to our services, enabling parents, students, and administrators to monitor bus locations, track student information, and receive important updates on-the-go.

Cloud Based Management

Our cloud-based management system provides dynamic and real-time updates of student information for enhanced safety and security during college bus commutes.

Benefits

  • Parental Peace of Mind: Parents can relax knowing they have real-time access to their child’s bus location, instant alerts for delays or incidents, and clear ETAs. This constant connectivity ensures their child’s safety and reduces daily worries.
